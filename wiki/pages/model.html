Modeling of dynamics

        Overview: The project is divided into four parts. First, the reverse transcription DNA amplification of lnk00857 is carried out by RAA technology. Second, the amplified DNA binds specifically to Cas protein to activate the collateral cleavage activity of Cas protein, and then cleaves the ssDNA that constitutes the hydrogel. Third, amylase in the DNA hydrogel is released, hydrolyzing the substrate to produce glucose. Fourth, the quantitative detection of lnk00857 is realized by reading the modified blood glucose meter. Models based on ordinary differential equations (Odes) are designed for the part involving enzymatic kinetics. The mathematical models are solved using R as a software tool.

Recombinase-aid amplification(RAA)

        Enzymic recombinant isothermal Amplification technology (ERA) is a new isothermal nucleic acid Amplification technology developed by Suzhou Xida Gene Company. The principle of the technology is basically the same with Recombinase Polymerase Amplification (RPA). The difference between the two is that the recombinant enzyme of RAA is obtained from bacteria or fungi, while the recombinant enzyme of RPA is derived from bacteriophage. Therefore, in this part, we will conduct modeling by changing some parameter values according to the dynamic equation in literature [1]. 1.Assumption -There is perfect symmetry between reactions involving forward and reverse primers. -ATP depletion was negligible over the time span examined. -The fluid is well mixed and all reactions that occur are kinetically controlled. 2.Overall reaction scheme diagram of RPA. 3.Response mechanism of RPA 4.Kinetic equation for RPA 5.Parameter 6.Reaction rate constant
