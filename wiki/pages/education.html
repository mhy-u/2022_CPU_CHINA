Primary School:Voluntary Support Education

In order to introduce synthetic biology to primary school students in poor areas of China, HP team members went to Shuping Town Primary School in Zhenping County, Ankang City, Shaanxi Province from 2022.07.19 to 2022.07.30 to introduce synthetic biology to primary school students in grades 4-6. We used a variety of approaches to popularize the science, hoping to teach as much knowledge as possible about synthetic biology and inspire their love for the subject in the limited time available.
1.CPU_CHINA members recorded a video for the students to reveal our daily laboratory routine. The video introduces the lab operation rules, the use of common lab instruments such as PCR instrument and centrifuge, and showes students some of the experiments in our project. Offline, we showed the video to the students in the classroom, and online, we uploaded the video to bilibili to expand the audience of the video through the new media platform.
2. CPU_CHINA members designed two courses for this education, with a total of three lessons. In order to help students understand synthetic biology better, we designed interactive sessions such as base pairing quiz and thinking about synthetic biology applications. In the second course, we held a mini-debate on "whether to support the development of gene editing technology" to develop children's dialectical thinking and provoke them to think about the ethics of synthetic biology. Both courses have been well received by students and have achieved good results.
3. CPU_CHINA members compiled science handbooks that accompany the content of the synthetic biology science course. Compared with the class content, the handbooks are more detailed and in-depth in explaining the basics of synthetic biology. We gave those handbooks to the students as gifts, and all the students liked them very much.
