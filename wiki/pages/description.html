Cancer detection

        Cancer is clinically known as malignant tumor, which progresses rapidly and has a high mortality rate. Cancer has caused great harm to patients' physical, psychological and economic conditions. Although not all cancers can be cured, early diagnosis often provides patients with the best chance of cure.

        With the implementation of various screening models, cancer detection can now be carried out before any obvious symptoms appear. Mammography, colonoscopy, Pap smear and low-dose CT scan can detect early breast cancer, colon cancer, cervical cancer and lung cancer respectively[1]. Tumor markers such as alpha fetoprotein (AFP) can also indicate the presence of tumors.

        Although the above screening tools can detect tumors at an early stage, most cancers are detected only when symptoms appear. This is mainly because the early symptoms of many cancers are not obvious, and people often ignore these mild symptoms. In addition, these cancer detection methods are very expensive and complex, and they can only detect one kind of cancer at a time, which really makes it very difficult to screen various types of cancer in advance. Moreover, many cancers still lack effective screening recommendations, and the benefits of screening are even minimal compared to potential hazards.

        Overall, screening for a variety of high-risk cancers is particularly important, as is simplifying cancer detection methods.

Township Health Center

        We hope to apply our products to township health centers.

        Township health centers are a comprehensive organization set up by counties or townships for health administration and medical prevention. The medical conditions of rural health centers are poor, and there is no more accurate testing equipment, so the villagers can only go to the more distant urban medical institutions for testing, which brings great inconvenience to their medical journey. Some people therefore cannot be tested in time, thus missing the best opportunity for treatment.

        In view of the above, we hope to provide villagers with a convenient and fast early screening equipment for a variety of cancers that can be used in township health centers, which can carry out cancer screening without large instruments, and provide strategies for their subsequent treatment.

Our solutions

        Our goal is toto develop a convenient and readable equipment for multiple cancers detection.

        To achieve our goal, we started to search for tumor biomarkers. One specific lincRNA, linc00857, caught our attention. And we further compared its expression in a great many tumor groups and normal group through the TCGA database and were surprised to find it highly correlate with the occurrence and progression of 22 cancers. Therefore, we assume that by detecting its expression, we could achieve the preliminary screening of these cancers and may forecast the future survival of patients.

        We then started to set up our system.We employed the recombinase polymerase amplification technique to selectively amplify blood samples, and generated nicking and strand displacement using nickase and polymerase, respectively. After processing the products into single-strand, we introduced CRISPR/Cas14 system into our kit, the smallest functional CRISPR system. The products of the strand displacement pair with crRNA to activate the nuclease domain of Cas14 to conduct cis-nonspecific cleavage of single-stranded DNA[2]. Based on this cleavage feature, we designed a hydrogel system that encapsulates the enzyme. The amount of the molecule and glucose produced are closely related, so the amount of glucose detected will reflect that of our molecule. In order to rapidly transfer electron generated by glucose oxidation to the surface of the electrode, we use an enzyme adsorption electrode glucose sensor as a means of detecting electrical signals and apply nanogold to redesign the existing glucose sensor electrode part. Compared with current qualitative or semi-quantitative assays based on color changing, our kit can achieve full quantification by reading sensor. This easy-operate kit perfectly fits township clinics or home testing in underdeveloped medical areas. Therefore, as long as the blood sample of patients is acquired, we can achieve rapid detection of them.
